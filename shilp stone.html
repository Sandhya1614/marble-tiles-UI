Our Story

Shilp Stones began in 1999 as a single yard outside Madurai, cutting Makrana and Rajasthan marble for local homes. Today we run our own polishing and CNC-edging floor, work directly with 18 quarry partners across India, Italy and the Middle East, and have completed over 2,600 residential and commercial installations.

Every slab that reaches your site has passed through our own inspection — for veining consistency, slab thickness and finish — before it ever appears in this catalog.
